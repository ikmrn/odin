Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Italian flavours in your dish.

This wonderful and simple recipe is a game changer for your evenings. Every member of your family is going to love this pasta. Its creamy texture and a touch of mediterrenian spices won't leave anyone indifferent.

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 1 package of mini farfale pasta

How to Make Cream Tuscan Chicken Pasta Step-By-Step

  1. Season both sides of chicken and place into how oil. Cook until golden.
  2. Reduce heat to low and add garlic, pour wine and cook until wine is almost evaporated
  3. Cook pasta
  4. Mix pasta with chicken thighs
  5. Let it rest before serving
